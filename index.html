Meet Deep Agents —
your AI that thinks, plans & acts.

Deep Agents Enhanced is a production-grade AI assistant built by Prathamesh Mishra using LangGraph and LangChain. Unlike a simple chatbot, it reasons step-by-step, searches the live internet via Tavily, coordinates specialist sub-agents for complex research and structured data extraction, reads and writes files in a virtual workspace, and streams every response token by token. Powered by GPT-4o, Llama 3, Qwen, and more — switch models in one click and watch the agent adapt.
