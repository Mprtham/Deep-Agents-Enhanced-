Meet Deep Agents —
your AI that thinks, plans & acts.

Deep Agents Enhanced is a production-grade AI assistant built by Prathamesh Mishra using LangGraph and LangChain. It reasons step-by-step, searches the live internet via Tavily, coordinates specialist sub-agents for complex research and structured data extraction, reads and writes files, and streams every response token by token. Switch between GPT-4o, Llama 3, Qwen and more — all in one click.
